VIT CGPA Calculator



VIT CGPA Calculator is a great tool that will help you calculate the CGPA for the present semester. Moreover VIT CGPA calculator also gives you the power to predict the CGPA for the coming Semesters. VIT CGPA Calcuator has been helping hundreds of students predict their CGPA. Hope it helps you :)


To begin with select the credits and the grades of the courses for which the results are out in the section 'Your details'.

If results are out for 5 courses the details should look like this(Note: Selecting all the courses in not binding.)

Now press the 'Get GPA' button.

Your GPA will be displayed.

Press the Calculate CGPA button to get the CGPA and fill the details.

The filled details looks as below , if you had campleted 102 credits with a cgpa of 8.8 last semester and this semester you had 24 credits and your GPA is 9.6. Press the 'Done' button to get the CGPA.

The CGPA will be displayed as shown.

You can get your CSE 1002 grades by pressing the 'Get CSE grade' button.
